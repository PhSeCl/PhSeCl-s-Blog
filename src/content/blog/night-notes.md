I like personal sites that reveal their rhythm before they explain themselves. Clear navigation matters, but mood matters too. A homepage can set the reading speed of everything that follows.

That is why this project leans so hard into spacing, gradients, motion, and silence. The point is not decoration for its own sake. The point is to make the writing feel like it belongs somewhere.

A small amount of atmosphere can make content easier to receive. When the page opens with the right tone, the text does not need to fight for attention. It can simply arrive.
